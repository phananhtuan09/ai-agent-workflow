## Formatting Notes

- When status is `Unverified` or `N/A`, replace the score with `Unverified` or `N/A` instead of fabricating numbers.
- `Observed` should stay factual.
- `Inference` should explain judgment, not repeat observation.
- If verdict strength is capped by coverage or confidence, say so explicitly.
