# Calibration Examples — Landing Page Showcase

Use these examples to calibrate judgment across models before assigning final scores.

These are not literal sites. They are reference patterns.

---

## Example A — Flashy But Weak

Typical profile:

- Dramatic hero with heavy glow, 3D, and layered gradients
- Many scroll effects and hover tricks
- Weak body typography
- Repetitive section structure after the hero
- Mobile looks cramped
- Runtime feels heavy

Likely range:

- Creative Direction: `3/5`
- Hero Impact: `4/5`
- Typography: `2/5`
- Storytelling / Flow: `2/5`
- Motion Design: `2/5` or `3/5`
- Performance: `1/5` or `2/5`
- Responsive Design: `2/5`

Why it is not top-tier:

- Flash does not replace pacing, readability, or runtime discipline.
- The hero creates a halo effect that should not inflate downstream categories.
- Motion looks expensive, but quality is capped by lack of restraint and smoothness.

---

## Example B — Strong Premium, Not Top Tier

Typical profile:

- Clear art direction and good brand feel
- Clean layout and well-crafted hero
- Motion is tasteful but not especially memorable
- Typography is solid, though not exceptional
- Mobile holds together well
- Runtime is good, not elite

Likely range:

- Creative Direction: `4/5`
- Brand Expression: `4/5`
- Hero Impact: `4/5`
- Typography: `3/5` or `4/5`
- Layout & Composition: `4/5`
- Motion Design: `3/5`
- Performance: `3/5`
- Responsive Design: `4/5`

Likely verdict:

- `80-89` range
- Strong premium quality

Why it stops short of top-tier:

- No weak fundamentals, but one or two signature qualities are missing.
- Motion and narrative progression are competent rather than exceptional.

---

## Example C — Real Top-Tier

Typical profile:

- Distinct concept visible from the first frame
- Hero is memorable without being noisy
- Section pacing is intentional and varied
- Typography is both expressive and highly readable
- Motion has clear hierarchy and remains smooth on realistic hardware
- Mobile feels designed, not merely adapted
- Technical execution feels invisible because nothing breaks immersion

Likely range:

- Creative Direction: `5/5`
- Brand Expression: `5/5`
- Hero Impact: `5/5`
- Typography: `4/5` or `5/5`
- Motion Design: `4/5` or `5/5`
- Performance: `4/5` or `5/5`
- Responsive Design: `4/5` or `5/5`

Why it qualifies:

- Beauty, smoothness, readability, and consistency all hold at once.
- The page stays impressive after the hero and under runtime pressure.

---

## Use This File To Resist Drift

When scores feel inflated:

- compare against Example A
- ask whether the page is only impressive in the hero
- ask whether runtime quality and readability actually keep up

When scores feel too conservative:

- compare against Example C
- ask whether the page truly combines design excellence with runtime discipline
